USA

The United States of America (USA) is a large and diverse country located in North America, bordered by Canada to the north and Mexico to the south. It is composed of 50 states, with Washington, D.C. as its capital. The USA is known for its cultural, economic, and political influence globally, and is a leader in technology, entertainment, and military power. It has a rich history shaped by immigration, a foundation of democracy, and significant achievements in science, space exploration, and civil rights. The country is also characterized by its varied geography, including mountains, forests, plains, and coastlines.
