    AVAILABLE TRIPS

    TRIP DESCRIPTION
    Beginner Trip: "Gentle Rapids Explorer" Perfect for families, first-time rafters, and those looking to enjoy a more relaxed pace on the water. The Gentle Rapids Explorer takes you down calm stretches of river, with a few gentle rapids to add just the right amount of excitement. Drift past beautiful scenery, wildlife, and learn the basics of paddling and river safety from our expert guides.
    Intermediate Trip: "Rogue River Rush" For those ready to take their rafting experience to the next level, the Rogue River Rush offers an exciting day on the water with Class III and IV rapids. Wind your way through narrow canyon passages and conquer heart-pounding waves while still having plenty of time to take in the stunning surroundings. This adventure includes a riverside lunch and moments of calm where you can swim or float, making it a perfect balance of thrill and relaxation.
    Advanced Trip: "The Adrenaline Cascade" Calling all adrenaline junkies! The Adrenaline Cascade is designed for seasoned rafters who crave a challenging and exhilarating adventure. You’ll tackle some of the most technical Class IV and V rapids. This expedition will take you deep into rugged wilderness, where you’ll experience steep drops, fast-moving water, and massive waves. After a day of battling the rapids, unwind by the campfire at a remote riverside campsite before diving back into the adventure the next day. This trip is the ultimate test of skill, stamina, and teamwork.
